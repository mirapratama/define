About Us


Define Magazine is a magazine published in JIS, which features the works of students in the areas of art and literature. It is a platform for student's art and literature to be recognized in JIS.

For each new volume, what we do is choose a unique theme. Once artists and writers have submitted their art/literary work, we compile and layout the images and texts to publish a new define magazine volume.
